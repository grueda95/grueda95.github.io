Adeventures in Bass Lake

Bass Lake

During the summer we love going to Bass Lake to relax and hike. Wether it be hiking up Angle pass or enjoying the sun and water at the lake it's always a good time with friends. Recently we visited and had a great time hiking. We hiked about 2 miles in the trail and ended up at an old wood cutting section of the trail where you could smell the trees really well. It also got so quiet that it felt unreal. Like there was no one around us and all you heard was the beat of your heart which was crazy.
