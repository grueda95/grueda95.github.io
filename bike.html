Riding My Bike

Biking

One of my favorite hobbies has been riding my bike. I feel much better after a 4 mile bike ride around town or in my neighborhood. Work and school can be stressful so after I'm done or if I can't concentrate I'll usually go on a ride. I have a vintage Peugot that I bought from someone that converted it into a single gear bike. It's much lighter than my last and it is faster as well. The colors are still in amazing condition espeically after the decades that it's been around.
