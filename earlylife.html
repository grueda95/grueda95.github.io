    • Blueberry Tree

    In my early life this is what I saw for a good part of it during my summers. We had to work in Oregon during the summers to make money for our clothes or other things we wanted as we grew up. From the earliest age of 10 I remmeber picking blueberries. My father passed away so we couldn't do much but support ourselves and my mother thought it was best that we all worked and saved up for what we wanted. She taught me to be the person who I am today and showed me what hard work really looks like. After all these years away from it I wouldn't step back into that work but I respect it with all my heart.
