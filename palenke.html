Palenke Arts Short Film

Palenke

This was a short film in which we joined an after school program to help with what we could and on top of that get to see what the program was about. The main person in this film was a young lady who was very passionate about being in her program and let us express that in our video. The kids were always happy to see us come by and acted natural as if cameras weren't there. This was an experience that helped us further push ourselves as well.
