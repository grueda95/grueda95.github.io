Yosemite

Yosemite

Yosemite was one of the greatest experiences I've had in awhile. I went for my birthday weekend and had lots of fun in the snow and cabin with all my good friends. Although, I can't forget about my trip when I was about 19 when I went with a couple of friends and had a terrible time. As we had hiked up to a waterfall the rain started coming down on us. We took refuge under a cliff of rocks hanging over but it wasn't enough and it seemed like water was going to overtake us so we ran towards a bathroom but it started hailing. On our way out the cliff hang I saved my friend from sliding off the side and that's when we knew we had to get out of there. We sprinted down the trails and eventually made it back. Gladly last year was just as eventful but not as dangerous.
